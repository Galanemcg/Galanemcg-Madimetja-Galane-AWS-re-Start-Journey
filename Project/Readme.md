**Taste Of Africa Restaurant Website**

Welcome to the official GitHub repository for Taste Of Africa, a restaurant website showcasing the rich flavors and traditions of African cuisine.

About

Taste Of Africa is a restaurant that celebrates the vibrant food culture of Africa, from the spices of Morocco to the bold flavors of West Africa. Our website is designed to provide an immersive experience, allowing customers to explore our menu, book tables, and order online.

Features

- Responsive Design: Our website is optimized for all devices, ensuring a seamless experience for customers.
- Menu Showcase: Browse our menu, featuring signature dishes and traditional African cuisine.
- Booking System: Book tables online, with real-time availability and confirmation.
- Order Online: Order your favorite dishes online, with secure payment processing.

Technologies Used

- HTML5: For structuring and presenting content.
- CSS3: For styling and layout.
- JavaScript: For interactive elements and dynamic functionality.
- Remix Icon: For icons and graphics.
